WD_BLACK Teamfight Tactics Competition

Date: 30/08/2019 Friday at 06:00 PM Dubai Time
Server: EUW
Discord: http://discord.gg/dr3tWa8

We are hosting a competition wtih WD_BLACK where fans will get to fight against each other in the League of Legends gamemode Teamfight Tactics and the winner will get to choose their prize, while the leftover prize will be given to one randomly chosen lucky winner!

Rules
  • Register by filling out the form below and by making sure you're a member of our discord and that you follow us on Twitter, and to retweet & like the Tweet!
  • Show up on the day of the competition and make sure you have Yash added on LoL (IGN: come tilt)
  • Games will be played in a BO1 format intially
  • Winners of the first round of games will compete in the finals round, where it will be a series of 3 games
  • The player will receive points based on their position at the end of the games (1st place = 1 point, 4th place = 4 points, etc)
  • The player with the least amount of points by the end of the 3 games will be declared the winner!
  • The winner gets to choose their prize, while the leftover prize will be given to one randomly chosen lucky winner
  • Finally, the winners can collect their prizes from the YaLLa Office!
Prizes
WD_BLACK SN750 500GB SSD
My Passport 4TB External HDD
